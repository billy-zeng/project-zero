Welcome to Stardust Road

We welcome you to this strange, faraway land where we find four adventurers stranded on what seems to be a never-ending rainbow path. Our weary travelers go by the names of Axel Arrondale, Jojo Johnson, Rudy Rambo, and Steven "Speedy" Shu. They come from different parts of the world and each carry their own pair of lucky dice. They've decided to play a little game to look for and collect fallen stars and they need your help!

First, you and a friend must each select an adventurer to play. You will take turns rolling your adventurers' lucky dice and your adventurer will move spaces on the board according to the results of the rolls. Landing on a green tile will advance you forward 1-2 spaces; Landing on a red tile will move you back 1-2 spaces; Landing on a yellow tile will give your adventurer 1-5 coins; Landing on a blue tile will allow your adventurer to visit the store and spend their coins. Your goal is to move your adventurer around the map and collect as many fallen stars as possible. When a star is picked up, a new one will appear on a random tile on the map. At the end of 30 rounds, the player with the most stars wins! Any remaining coins will also be converted to stars at a 15:1 ratio. Best of luck!
